StockSmart Site Plan

Site Name

StockSmart - This name reflects a site where users can track global stocks and money market investments in one place. It's easy to remember, brandable, and directly speaks to the app's core benefit: helping users stay focused on their finances.

Site Purpose

This website will provide a simple dashboard to track foreign stocks, money market yields, and financial news relevant to global investors. It will offer educational content, a tracker, and possibly a weekly spotlight on stock opportunities and safe cash investment ideas.

Scenarios

  • What are the best global stocks to invest in right now for growth or income?
  • How can I keep my idle cash safe while earning passive income from dollar-based money markets?

Color Scheme

  • #005b96 - Used for headings, navigation links, and key call-to-action buttons.
  • #f37022 - Used for accent highlights, icons, and active elements like selected cards or alerts.
  • #f8f9fa - Light background to keep the interface clean and easy on the eyes.

Typography

  • Playfair Display - Used for main headings and key titles to give the site a premium, elegant feel.
  • Inter - Used for body text, navigation links, and general content. It's clean and easy to read on screens.
